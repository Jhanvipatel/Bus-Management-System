

ABOUT US:-

Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation (GSRTC) is a passenger transport 
organisation providing bus services both within Gujarat and neighbouring states.

GSRTC came into existence on 1st May, 1960 on formation of Gujarat. From a 
modest begining of 7 divisions, 76 depots and 7 divisional workshops and 
a fleet of 1,767 buses it has gone to,

--   16 Divisions
--   126 Depots
--   226 bus stations
--   1,554 pick up stands
--   8,000 buses

This remarkable growth is an outcome of unflagging effort of more than 50,000 
workforce, dynamic management and sustained support from the state govt. 
It has built up formidable technical facilities.
